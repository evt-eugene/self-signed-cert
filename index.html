Certificate Formats

PEM Bundle (.pem file)

A combined certificate and key file, typically used in Apache or Nginx servers. The PEM format is widely supported and secure.

PEM (.crt and .key files)

Separate certificate and key files, generally used for web servers such as Apache and Nginx. Commonly preferred for its compatibility and simplicity.

PFX (.pfx file)

Contains both certificate and private key in a secure format, commonly used in Windows-based systems like IIS. It can also be password protected.

PKCS#12 (.p12 file)

Similar to PFX, this format is used in Windows/IIS and includes both the certificate and private key. Often used for importing certificates into browsers or servers.
